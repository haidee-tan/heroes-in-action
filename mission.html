The Mission

There are those big time heroes who died for the sake of others. But there are those who sacrifice yet made ripples of change affecting people around them.

These are the heroes we encounter everyday.

We want the world know who they are and how they trigger inspiration to the people around them. And it's not because they long for fame; it's not recognition that drives a hero after all.

It's because we want people to follow their trail, in the hope that one day, they'll make their own.

because we all need heroes.
